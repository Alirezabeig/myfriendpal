Welcome to Pal! We are thrilled you decided to use our services. We take your privacy extremely seriously and are committed to safeguarding your personal information.

What Information We Collect

We also collect data regarding how you interact with our services. This can include:

We employ stringent security measures and store your information on secure AWS servers. All data is encrypted and regular security audits are performed.

Your information is confidential. We do not share it with any third parties. Additionally, no member of the company has access to your confidential information.

We will retain your information for as long as your account is active. Should you choose to delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed.

Your Rights

You have various rights regarding your personal data, including:

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Updates will be reflected on this page and noted by a change in the "Last Updated" date at the top of the policy.
